How to play ?

  • Toggle the switches for background music and sound effects for the best game experience (Only available on PC).
  • To start the game fill your name in the text field below, your name cannot contain any spaces.
  • Remember, you have got only 30 seconds per question, your time will start right away as you are in the hot seat and timer is displayed in top right corner.
  • Your progress is indicated in the progress bar on the left.
  • You can avail of three lifelines :
    • Extra time (You will get 30 extra seconds)
    • Change the question (Different question will be displayed)
    • 50:50 (Two incorrect answers will be removed)
  • The score is counted the following way :
    • Each question has a weight between € 5 and € 20.000
    • After answering the question correctly, the weight is multiplied by the time left.
    • Score for each question is added to the final score figure.
    • Current score is displayed in top left corner.
  • There're 10 questions in total, with 3 different levels of difficulty
    • Questions 1-3 : Easy
    • Questions 4-6 : Medium
    • Questions 4-6 : Hard
    • Final Question 10 : Super Hard
  • The game is over when :
    • You run out of time on any question - no win.
    • You answer wrong any of the questions - no win.
    • You answer all questions correctly - you will be winner.
  • If you win the game, your score will be recorded in the leader board in your local browser.
  • Enjoy the game ;)
